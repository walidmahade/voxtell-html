FULL LIST OF FEATURES

A Complete Phone Service Package Without The Hassle

Discover all the features that will make your life easier and your customers happier

Admin Portal A dedicated portal for account managers that lets you control all functions of your phone system.
Busy Lamp Field Indicates which phones are in use by displaying a status on your phone display. BLF keys also act as a speed dial.
Call Announce Call Announce whispers an audible version of the custom tag to you before the call is connected.
Call Blocking Block calls based on an area code, number or other options like 411 or international calls.
Call Conference (3 Way Calling) Add a second outgoing call to an already connected call.
Call Continuity You’ll never have service interruption — even if you lose power or internet.
Call Switch Easily move ongoing calls between your desk phone and mobile device (and vice-versa).
Call Forwarding Redirect calls to your mobile phone or other phone so you don’t miss any calls.
Call Hold Easily put a call on hold while you answer another call.
Call Logs Provides detailed business call records by extension or account.
Call Park ‘Park’ a call in a ‘spot’ to be retrieved by another extension.
Call Screening Know who’s calling you before you answer. Send call to voicemail or transfer before you speak to the caller.
Call Transfer Attended Transfer (alert forwarding party before transfer) & Blind Transfer (transfer the call directly).
Call Waiting Be notified when someone else is trying to call if you are already on a call.
Set Caller ID This feature provides options for setting Caller ID numbers and, if preferred, alpha-numeric names that appear on outbound calls for each extension.
Shared Line Appearance Monitor the extensions of others on your account and pick up or park those calls as desired.
Simultaneous Ring Configure your cell phone or other phone to also ring as call comes in.
Text Messaging (Coming Soon) Send and receive texts using your Voxtell Business phone number via the Voxtell Portal app.
Seven-Digit Dialing et a default area code to make dialing even quicker
Voicemail Securely check your messages from anywhere, even online.
Voicemail to Email Receive voicemail as an .WAV file (audio file) attached to email.
Work from Anywhere Access phone service on any device, anywhere: smartphone, tablet, computer. Even plug your office IP phone into high-speed internet at home.
Caller ID Know who’s calling before you answer.
Caller ID Block Block your caller ID from being displayed on outbound calls.
Cell Phone Integration Forward calls to cell phones and use functions from cell phones.
Click-to-Dial Seamlessly integrate Voxtell service with your web browser to initiate calls with Click-to-Dial.
Custom Call Tagging Easily identify origin of incoming calls by adding a unique tag to each published phone number.
Dashboard Provides real time status of all extensions within your cloud.
Directory Assistance (411) Gather information from listings. (Additional fees apply)
Do Not Disturb Callers go directly to voicemail when you don’t want to be disturbed.
Dynamic Caller ID Set any phone number on your account as your caller ID.
Emergency Assistance (911) Set an address for individual extensions or your entire account so that responders know where to send help in the event of an emergency.
End User Portal Administrators and users configure and manage their own business service through an easy-to-use web interface.
Follow Me Calls can be forwarded to multiple numbers that will ring in designated sequence.
Multiple Devices on One Extension Register up to three devices on one Voxtell Business extension.
Music on Hold Upload your own music or customized recording.
Never Miss a Call Customize call settings so you always stay connected no matter where you are.
Phone Number Porting Keep an existing phone number by transferring it to your account.
